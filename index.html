Our Vision

At Government School for Girls, our vision is to provide quality education to every child, ensuring that learning reaches every home and community. We aim to empower our students with knowledge, skills, and values that help them grow academically and personally, fostering responsible, confident, and capable future citizens.

Subjects Offered

Our school provides a comprehensive curriculum designed to nurture knowledge, creativity, and critical thinking in every student.

Mathematics

Develop problem-solving skills, analytical thinking, and a strong foundation in numbers and logic.

Science

Explore the world of physics, chemistry, and biology with hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning.

English

Enhance reading, writing, and communication skills while fostering creativity and critical thinking.

Social Studies

Learn about history, geography, and civic responsibilities to become informed and responsible citizens.

Computer Science

Introduction to coding, digital literacy, and technology skills essential for the modern world.

Arts & Crafts

Encouraging creativity and self-expression through drawing, painting, and various craft activities.
